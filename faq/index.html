Safety Precautions Relating to Covid-19 and other illnesses

To create a safer environment and to mitigate the spread of Covid-19 virus and other influenza, there will be extra steps taking to ensure safety for patient and practitioner.

  • Masks and/or shield will be worn by practitioner
  • All patients are required to wear a mask to their appointment IF they have a cough. If your cough is getting worse leading up to your appointment please let us know or reschedule if possible.
  • Hand washing and sanitizing of equipement and common surfaces before and after treatment
  • Contactless payment is available through the booking software prior to appointment (Standard payment of debit/credit is avaialble post-treatment)


What to know PRIOR to your Appointment

You must fill out your Health History Form and Consent sent via JaneAPP - if you did not receive this email, please let us know and we'd be happy to assist you.

During treament, the practitioner wil be moving the patient; Patients should arrive wearing clothing that will not restrict your movement.

A mask is manditory and must be worn the full duration of your appointment.



What to know for the DAY OF your Appointment

Please call ahead if you are experiencing any Covid-19-like symptoms or from other illnesses such as fever, difficulty breathing or swalloing, sudden onset cough, or any other symptoms mentioned in the survery (cancellation fees will be waived in this instance).

  • Arrive 5 minutes earlier and wait in your vehicle until 1 minute prior to your appointment.
  • Sanitize your hands with provided sanitizer BEFORE and AFTER treatment


Is Manual Osteopathy safe?

With any type of manual therapy there is some inherant risk but no more than other forms of manual therapies. Osteopathy is relatively gentle and non-aggressive form of treatment that does not involve forcing and being aggressive with the patient. We pride ourselves in our thought process and rationale to guide our treatment to create the most amount of positive change, with the least amount of stress through the patient's body.

    More Gain - Less Pain.


What can Osteopathy help with?

Manual Osteopathic treatment aims to restore proper communication with the arteries, veins, nerve and lymphatic systems by improving motion.

Osteopathy can help with problems you may have relating to these systems, such as:

  • Chronic and Acute Muscle & Joint Pain
  • Joint Mobility
  • Headaches
  • TMJ(jaw) & Neck Issues
  • Digestive Concerns
  • Improved Circulation
  • ...and more!

By improving the communication, we let the body self-heal and regulate and improve the natural functions of the body.

With osteopathy, motion is used to make an anatomical connection to the physiology (what you're experiencing). By restoring proper anatomical motion and alignment, we allow the blood, nerve and lymphatic systems surrounding the area to function more optimally.
